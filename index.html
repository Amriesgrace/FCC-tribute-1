About

Gordon James Ramsay also known as Gordon, Gordon Ramsay, or GR is a British Chef, restauranteur and TV personality.

Gordon Ramsay is a Scottish born British chef with his restaurants currently holding 16 Michelin stars. Internationally renowned, Multi-Michelin starred chef Gordon Ramsay has multiple of successful restaurants across the world

Gordon has also become a star of the TV screen internationally, with shows such as Kitchen Nightmares, Hellâ€™s Kitchen, Hotel Hell and MasterChef USA.
